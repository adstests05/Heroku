Confirm Before you Believe

Fake news is a type of yellow journalism or propaganda that consists of deliberate disinformation via traditional print and broadcast news media or online social media. The false information is often caused by reporters paying sources for stories, an unethical practice called checkbook journalism. The real impact of the growing interest in fake news has been the realization that the public might not be well-equipped to separate quality information from false information.


So we are using Machine learning techniques to find the integrity of the news from the Newspaper articles. We are doing them in three different ways. Input can be given to the site through the following methods


1. Through URL 2. Content 3. Current Date Newspaper
